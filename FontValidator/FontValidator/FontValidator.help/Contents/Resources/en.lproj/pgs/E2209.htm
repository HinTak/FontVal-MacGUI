Message: E2209

PCLT: The SerifStyle top 2 bit value is invalid

The most significant two bits of the serifStyle byte specify the serif/sans or contrast/monoline characterisitics of the typeface. The bits may have the following values:

0 reserved
1 Sans Serif/Monoline
2 Serif/Contrasting
3 reserved

For more information, see the PCLT table specification.
